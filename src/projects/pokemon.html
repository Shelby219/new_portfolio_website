TLDR; Click the Pokeball to generate a random pokemon card to add to your collection!

Pokecard Generator was created to practice working with fetch and Express JS.

Each time the ball is clicked, data is fetched from the PokeAPI and adds a card to the screen for collection.

Future to do's include improve the animations, and expand the application to be a user based trading card application.
